Dive map

Dokdo not only lies far from the mainland but also sits at the boundary where warm and cold ocean currents meet, giving it a unique geopolitical characteristic. This results in a distinct marine ecosystem different from that of the East Sea coast or Ulleungdo. Dokdo maintains the pristine and healthy conditions of the past East Sea, earning it the highest accolades such as "golden fishing grounds" and "treasure trove of biodiversity."
